Helpful Resources

What to Play

DriveThruRPG is a website selling digital and physical copies of hundreds of TTRPG books and other resources.

Where to Play

Roll20 is an online virtual tabletop where players can gather to play many different TTRPGs from the comfort of their home.

Who to Game With

The LFG subreddit is a great place to find new or ongoing games to join in many different TTRPG games.
